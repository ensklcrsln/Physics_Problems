# 8. Variable Force Work

## Problem

F = -kx

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Equation of motion

m d²x/dt² = -kx

This is SHM.

---

### Step 2: Work

W = ∫ -kx dx

W = -(1/2)kx²

---

### Step 3: Potential energy

U = (1/2)kx²

F = -dU/dx ✔

---

### Interpretation

Energy is stored in the system.
