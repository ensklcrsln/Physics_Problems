# 3. Conservation of Energy (Pendulum)

## Problem

L = 1 m, θ = 15°

Find speed at bottom.

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Height difference

h = L(1 - cosθ)

cos15° ≈ 0.966

h = 1 × (1 - 0.966) = 0.034 m

---

### Step 2: Energy conservation

mgh = (1/2)mv²

Cancel m:

gh = (1/2)v²

Solve:

v = sqrt(2gh)

v = sqrt(2 × 9.81 × 0.034)

v = sqrt(0.667)

v ≈ 1.6 m/s

---

### Interpretation

All potential energy converts into kinetic energy at the bottom.
