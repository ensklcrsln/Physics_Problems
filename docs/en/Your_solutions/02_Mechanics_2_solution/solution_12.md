# 12. Work & Energy

## Problem

F = (6,2), m = 2 kg

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Acceleration

a = F/m = (3,1)

---

### Step 2: Velocity

v = (3t + 1, t - 1)

---

### Step 3: Position

r = (1.5t² + t, 0.5t² - t)

---

### Step 4: Work

At t = 3:

r(3) = (16.5, 1.5)

W = F · r

W = 6 × 16.5 + 2 × 1.5

W = 102 J

---

### Step 5: Energy check

Work equals change in kinetic energy ✔
