## 10. Force Field and Power

### Given:

$$
x = 5t^2 - t,\quad y = 2t^3,\quad z = -3t + 2
$$

---

### Step 1: Velocity

$$
v = (10t - 1,\ 6t^2,\ -3)
$$

---

### Step 2: Acceleration

$$
a = (10,\ 12t,\ 0)
$$

---

### Step 3: Force

$$
F = ma = (5,\ 6t,\ 0)
$$

---

### Step 4: Power

$$
P = F \cdot v = 50t - 5 + 36t^3
$$

---

### Explanation:

- Velocity is the derivative of position.
- Acceleration is the derivative of velocity.
- Power measures energy transfer rate.

---

### Formula 1: Velocity

$$
v = \frac{dr}{dt}
$$

---

### Formula 2: Acceleration

$$
a = \frac{dv}{dt}
$$

---

### Formula 3: Newton’s Second Law

$$
F = ma
$$

---

### Formula 4: Linear Momentum

$$
p = mv
$$

---

### Formula 5: Power

$$
P = F \cdot v
$$
