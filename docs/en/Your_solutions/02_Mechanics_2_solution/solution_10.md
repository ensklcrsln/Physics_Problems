# 10. Force Field & Power

## Problem

x = 5t² - t  
y = 2t³  
z = -3t + 2  
m = 0.5 kg  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Velocity

v = (10t - 1, 6t², -3)

---

### Step 2: Acceleration

a = (10, 12t, 0)

---

### Step 3: Force

F = ma

F = (5, 6t, 0)

---

### Step 4: Power

P = F · v

P = 5(10t - 1) + 6t(6t²)

P = 50t - 5 + 36t³
