# 1. Gravitational Dependence

## 📊 Summary

| Quantity | Result |
|----------|--------|
| Moon period | ≈ 9.8 s |
| Length (T=1s) | ≈ 0.25 m |

---

## 🧠 Step-by-step

### 1. Formula

T = 2π sqrt(L / g)

---

### 2. Moon case

g_moon = g / 6

T ∝ 1 / sqrt(g)

So:

T_moon = 4 × sqrt(6) ≈ 4 × 2.45 ≈ 9.8 s

---

### 3. Length for T = 1 s

1 = 2π sqrt(L / g)

Square both sides:

1 = 4π² (L / g)

Solve:

L = g / (4π²) ≈ 9.81 / 39.48 ≈ 0.25 m
