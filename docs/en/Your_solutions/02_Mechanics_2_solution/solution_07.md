# 7. Friction Dynamics

## Problem

m1 = 5 kg (fixed)  
m2 = 10 kg (moving)  
F = 45 N  
μ = 0.2  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Friction

f = μmg

f = 0.2 × 5 × 9.81 = 9.8 N

---

### Step 2: Net force

F_net = 45 - 9.8 = 35.2 N

---

### Step 3: Acceleration

a = F / m

a = 35.2 / 10

a = 3.52 m/s²

---

### Interpretation

Friction reduces acceleration.
