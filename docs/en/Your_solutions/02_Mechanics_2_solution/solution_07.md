# 7. Dynamics with Friction

## 📊 Result

a ≈ 3.52 m/s²

---

## 🧠 Solution

Friction always opposes motion.

---

### Friction force

f = μmg = 0.2 × 5 × 9.81 ≈ 9.8 N

---

### Net force

F_net = 45 - 9.8 = 35.2 N

---

### Acceleration

a = F/m = 35.2 / 10 ≈ 3.52 m/s²

👉 Friction reduces acceleration.
