# 2. Harmonic Motion

## 📊 Summary

| Quantity | Result |
|----------|--------|
| k | ≈ 9869 N/m |
| Energy | ≈ 197 J |

---

## 🧠 Explanation

Given:

x(t) = 0.2 cos(10πt)

Angular frequency:

ω = 10π

k = mω² = 10 × (10π)² ≈ 9869

---

Energy:

E = (1/2)kA²

= 0.5 × 9869 × (0.2)² ≈ 197 J
