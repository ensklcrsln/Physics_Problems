# 2. Harmonic Motion

## Problem

x(t) = 0.2 cos(10πt), m = 10 kg

Find:
- Spring constant k
- Total energy

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Identify SHM form

General form:

x(t) = A cos(ωt)

So:

A = 0.2 m  
ω = 10π rad/s  

---

### Step 2: Spring constant

Formula:

k = mω²

Compute:

ω² = (10π)² = 100π²

k = 10 × 100π² = 1000π²

π² ≈ 9.87

k ≈ 9869 N/m

---

### Interpretation

Higher frequency means stronger restoring force → stiffer spring.

---

### Step 3: Energy

Formula:

E = (1/2)kA²

Substitute:

A² = 0.04

E = 0.5 × 9869 × 0.04

E ≈ 197 J

---

### Interpretation

Energy stays constant during oscillation.
