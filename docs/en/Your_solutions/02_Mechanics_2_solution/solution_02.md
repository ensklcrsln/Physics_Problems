# 2. Harmonic Motion

## 📊 Summary

| Quantity | Result |
|----------|--------|
| k | ≈ 9869 N/m |
| Energy | ≈ 197 J |

---

## 🧠 Explanation

Given:

x(t) = 0.2 cos(10πt)

Compare with:

x = A cos(ωt)

So:

- Amplitude A = 0.2  
- Angular frequency ω = 10π  

---

### Spring constant

k = mω²

➡ Higher frequency → stiffer spring

---

### Energy

E = (1/2)kA²

➡ Energy is constant in SHM  
➡ Depends only on amplitude
