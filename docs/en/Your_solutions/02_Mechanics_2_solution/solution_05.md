# 5. Inelastic Collision

## Problem

m1 = 70 kg, v1 = 3 m/s  
m2 = 140 kg, v2 = 0  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Momentum

70 × 3 = 210

210 = (70 + 140)v

210 = 210v

v = 1 m/s

---

### Step 2: Energy check

Initial:

E = (1/2)(70)(9) = 315 J

Final:

E = (1/2)(210)(1) = 105 J

---

### Interpretation

Energy is not conserved because it transforms into heat and deformation.
