## 7. Logic & Series (The Fly)

### Step 1: Time to reach the wall

$$
t = \frac{\text{distance}}{\text{speed}} = \frac{10}{1} = 10 \text{ s}
$$

### Step 2: Fly motion

The fly moves continuously at:

$$
v = 2 \text{ m/s}
$$

### Step 3: Total distance

$$
\text{distance} = v \cdot t
$$

$$
\text{distance} = 2 \cdot 10 = 20
$$

### Result

Total distance traveled:

$$
20 \text{ meters}
$$
