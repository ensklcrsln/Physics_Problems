# 8. Circular Motion

Given:

Earth radius:

R = 6378 km = 6.378 × 10<sup>6</sup> m

---

## 📊 Summary Table

| Quantity | Expression | Result |
|----------|------------|--------|
| Angular velocity | ω = 2π / T | 7.27 × 10⁻⁵ |
| Period | T = 86400 s | Given |
| Acceleration | a = ω²R | ≈ 0.034 m/s² |

---

## 🧠 Step-by-step explanation

### 1. Formula

Centripetal acceleration:

a = ω²R

---

### 2. Angular velocity

Earth rotates once per day:

T = 86400 s

ω = 2π / T ≈ 7.27 × 10⁻⁵

---

### 3. Substitute

a = (7.27 × 10⁻⁵)² × 6.378 × 10⁶

≈ 0.034 m/s²

---

## 📌 Key insight

- Very small acceleration  
- Much smaller than gravity (9.81 m/s²)  
- That’s why we don’t feel it
