## 8. Circular Motion

### Given:

- Radius of Earth $R = 6378$ km  
- Rotation period $T = 24$ hours  

---

### Step 1: Linear speed

$$
v = \frac{2 \pi R}{T} \approx 463 \text{ m/s}
$$

---

### Step 2: Centripetal acceleration

$$
a_c = \frac{v^2}{R} \approx 0.0336 \text{ m/s}^2
$$

---

### Explanation:

- Centripetal acceleration keeps object in circular motion.  
- Compare to gravity: small but measurable.
