## 3. Path Intersection (Alice & Bob)

### 🎯 Idea

We compare positions at same time t.

If:
A(t) = B(t)
then they intersect.

---

## 📌 Step 1: Write equations

Alice:
A(t) = (2 + t, 8 − 3t)

Bob:
B(t) = (2t − 1, 2t + 2)

---

## 📌 Step 2: Why equate components?

Because:
👉 Two vectors are equal only if x and y are both equal

So we solve:

2 + t = 2t − 1  
8 − 3t = 2t + 2

---

## 📌 Step 3: Solve system

From first:
t = 3

Check second:
8 − 9 = 2(3) + 2 →  -1 ≠ 8

So inconsistent.

---

## 📌 Conclusion

No intersection → no collision.
