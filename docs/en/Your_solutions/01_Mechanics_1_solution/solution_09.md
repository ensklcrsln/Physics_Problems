## 9. Momentum Comparison

### Given:

- Fly: mass = 2 g, speed = 10 m/s  
- Tennis ball: mass = 60 g, speed = 1 m/s  

---

### Step 1: Convert mass

- Fly: 0.002 kg  
- Ball: 0.06 kg  

---

### Step 2: Momentum

$$
p_{fly} = 0.002 \cdot 10 = 0.02
$$

$$
p_{ball} = 0.06 \cdot 1 = 0.06
$$

---

### Step 3: Comparison

- Tennis ball has larger momentum.  

---

### Formula: Momentum

$$
p = m v
$$
