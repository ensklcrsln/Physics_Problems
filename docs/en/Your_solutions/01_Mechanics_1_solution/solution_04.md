## 4. Vector Calculus

Given:

r(t) = (3t^2)i + (5t − 8t^2)j

---

Why differentiate?

We differentiate because:
- velocity is change of position over time
- acceleration is change of velocity over time

---

Velocity:

v(t) = dr/dt

v(t) = (6t)i + (5 − 16t)j

---

Acceleration:

a(t) = dv/dt

a(t) = (6)i + (−16)j

---

Interpretation:
- acceleration is constant
- motion is not uniform
