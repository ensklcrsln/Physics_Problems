## 4. Vector Calculus

Given:

r(t) = (3t²)i + (5t − 8t²)j

---

## 🧠 Why differentiate?

Because:
- velocity = rate of change of position
- acceleration = rate of change of velocity

---

## 📌 Velocity

v(t) = dr/dt

v(t) = (6t)i + (5 − 16t)j

---

## 📌 Acceleration

a(t) = dv/dt

a(t) = (6)i + (−16)j

---

## 📌 Interpretation

- acceleration is constant
- motion is non-uniform
