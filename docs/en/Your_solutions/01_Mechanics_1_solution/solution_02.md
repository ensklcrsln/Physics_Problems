## 2. Range Optimization

### 🎯 Goal

We want to maximize:

R(θ) = (v₀² sin(2θ)) / g

---

## 🧠 Why does angle matter?

Because:
- sin(2θ) controls range
- v₀ and g are constants

So optimization depends only on sin(2θ)

---

## 📌 Step 1: Maximize sine function

We know:
- Maximum value of sine is 1

So:

sin(2θ) = 1

---

## 📌 Step 2: Solve angle

2θ = 90°

θ = 45°

---

## 📌 Final conclusion

Maximum range occurs at:

θ = 45°
